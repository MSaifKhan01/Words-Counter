About Word Count App

Welcome to the Word Count App, your go-to tool for analyzing the content of any webpage. Our application is designed to provide you with valuable insights about the text and images on a website. Here's how it works:

Word Count Analysis

The Word Count App allows you to enter the URL of a webpage. It then extracts the text content and provides you with the total word count. You can use this feature to get a quick overview of the textual content on any website.

Image Insights

Our app also retrieves the image URLs from the webpage, helping you identify the visual elements present. This feature is especially useful for understanding the media content of a website.

Favorite Insights

You can mark insights as favorites to keep track of important findings. This feature allows you to save and organize valuable information for future reference.

Search History

Word Count App maintains a history of your previous searches, making it easy to revisit and manage your insights. You can also remove entries from your history as needed.

Experience the power of the Word Count App and gain meaningful insights into the webpages you explore. Start using our application today!
